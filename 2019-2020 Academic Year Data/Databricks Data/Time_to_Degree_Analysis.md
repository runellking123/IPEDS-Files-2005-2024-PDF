# 2019-2020 Academic Year - Time-to-Degree Analysis

**Data Source:** Wiley University Databricks (Jenzabar J1 Database)
**Query Date:** December 16, 2025
**Period:** July 1, 2019 - June 30, 2020

---

## Overview

This analysis examines the time required for bachelor's degree completion, measuring from initial entry date to degree conferral date.

---

## Time to Bachelor's Degree Completion

| Time Category | Graduates | Percentage |
|---------------|-----------|------------|
| < 4 years | 126 | 75.9% |
| 4-5 years | 27 | 16.3% |
| 5-6 years | 3 | 1.8% |
| 6-7 years | 5 | 3.0% |
| 7+ years | 5 | 3.0% |

**Total Analyzed:** 166 bachelor's degree recipients with entry date data

---

## Completion Rate Analysis

### Traditional 4-Year Track
- **Under 4 years:** 126 graduates (75.9%)
- Most students in this category are likely transfer students with prior credits

### Extended Completion (4-6 Years)
- **4-5 years:** 27 graduates (16.3%)
- **5-6 years:** 3 graduates (1.8%)
- **Combined 4-6 years:** 30 graduates (18.1%)

### Long-Term Completers (6+ Years)
- **6-7 years:** 5 graduates (3.0%)
- **7+ years:** 5 graduates (3.0%)
- **Combined 6+ years:** 10 graduates (6.0%)

---

## Key Insights

- **Majority complete quickly:** 75.9% finish in under 4 years (likely transfer students)
