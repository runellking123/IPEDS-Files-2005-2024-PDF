## Key Observations

**Strengths:**
- Competitive overall graduation rate (36% vs. 32% median)
- Strong institutional grant aid provision ($8,319 vs. $6,215 median)
- Lower tuition costs than peer institutions
- High Pell grant participation (99% of students)

**Areas of Focus:**
- Lower full-time retention rate (51% vs. 59% median)
- Lower 4- and 6-year bachelor's degree completion rates
- Salary competitiveness for faculty, particularly at higher ranks
- Shift toward digital resources needed (currently 48% vs. 67% median)
